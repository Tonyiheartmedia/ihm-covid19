A Note on Returning to Offices

Friday, May 1, 2020

I hope you and your loved ones are safe and in good health. As we all begin to hear news of the states and businesses re-opening, we want to update you on our company’s plans for returning to our offices.

Our number one goal is to maximize the safety of our employees as we begin to return to our offices. Although we will have a consistent fact based and phased approach for the reopening of all of our locations with specific reopening protocols, they will be customized to the different circumstances in each of our 160 business locations. As individual plans are being built and rolled out, you should know that we are using input from government agencies including the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) to guide our timing of openings and our related protocols.

We also want to include input from you. Therefore, we will be conducting an employee survey next week that will help us with these important decisions. Please watch for the survey in your email box – it will be coming from COVID-19 SURVEY – and be sure to take a few minutes to respond; your answers will be important in helping us craft the right approach that both protects your health and supports our business.

  • The link to the survey is your unique individual link. It should not be used to take the survey on behalf of anyone else.

  • You must use the link sent to you in the invite email. The link should not be shared or forwarded to anyone else.

Finally, the current protocols are still in place and should stay in effect until further notice:

  • All iHeartMedia locations should continue operating with only essential employees in the office until further notice unless notified otherwise by your Market President.

  • No business travel unless absolutely necessary and pre-approved in writing by your senior management group leader. Keep in mind that the CDC and certain state guidelines recommend isolation following travel to certain locations.

  • No outside visitors are allowed into iHeartMedia locations, except for those pre-approved by the Market President.

  • If you are an essential employee and you are coming into the office, please remember to practice social distancing and DO NOT come to work if you are sick

Updates will be provided as we roll out and adjust our plans for reopening offices. Please help us ensure the safety of our broadcast operations and essential employees who are unable to work remotely by following these company protocols.

Keep yourself safe and healthy!

We want to keep you safe during this period of time regarding the Coronavirus. We’ve encouraged all employees to follow responsible health procedures to protect yourself and the people around you. Below please find the measures to help protect your health.

Wash your hands

frequently and use hand sanitizer throughout the day.

Avoid touching your mouth, nose, and eyes

Sneeze or cough into your elbow, not your hands, and avoid shaking hands.

Minimize or Eliminate

exposure to public transit.

Stay home

If you have even mild cold or flu symptoms

Avoid

International travel.

Consider your own contingency planning

Check your emergency supplies and ensure you have adequate food, medicine (in particular, prescription medicine), and critical household goods (think batteries, trash bags, hygiene supplies, etc.) to last at least 30 days.
